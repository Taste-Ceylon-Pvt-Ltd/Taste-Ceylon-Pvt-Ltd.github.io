About Alba Grade Ceylon Cinnamon

Alba Grade represents the pinnacle of Ceylon cinnamon quality. Named for its light, "alba" (white) color, this premium grade features quills with a diameter of less than 6mm, making them the thinnest and most delicate available.

What Makes Alba Grade Special?

Each Alba quill is hand-peeled by skilled artisans who have perfected their craft over generations. The paper-thin layers are carefully rolled and dried to preserve the essential oils that give Ceylon cinnamon its distinctive sweet, complex flavor with subtle citrus undertones.

Health Benefits

True Ceylon cinnamon contains significantly lower coumarin levels compared to common cassia cinnamon, making it safer for regular consumption. Studies suggest Ceylon cinnamon may help:

  • Support healthy blood sugar levels
  • Provide antioxidant properties
  • Support digestive health
  • Add warmth and flavor without added sugar

Storage Recommendations

Store in an airtight container away from direct sunlight, heat, and moisture. Properly stored, whole cinnamon quills maintain their quality for 3-4 years.
