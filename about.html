The Taste Ceylon Journey

Founded with a passion for authentic flavors, Taste Ceylon began as a small family venture dedicated to sharing the genuine taste of Sri Lankan cinnamon with the world. What started in the lush plantations of Sri Lanka's central highlands has grown into a trusted name in premium spices.

Our founders, descendants of cinnamon cultivators, recognized that the world deserved access to true Ceylon cinnamon ‚Äî not the common cassia often sold as cinnamon. This commitment to authenticity drives everything we do.

Today, we work directly with over 200 small-scale farmers across Sri Lanka, ensuring fair prices while maintaining the highest quality standards. Every quill is hand-peeled, every powder freshly ground, preserving the delicate flavors that have made Ceylon cinnamon legendary.
