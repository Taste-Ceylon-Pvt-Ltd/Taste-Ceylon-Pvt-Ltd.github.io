Products & Quality

Ceylon cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum) is the "true" cinnamon, native to Sri Lanka. It has a lighter, sweeter flavor with citrus notes, and contains significantly lower levels of coumarin compared to cassia cinnamon (commonly sold in supermarkets). Ceylon cinnamon quills are also distinguishable by their paper-thin layers that can be easily crumbled.
All our cinnamon is sourced directly from certified farms in Sri Lanka's cinnamon-growing regions. Each product comes with a certificate of authenticity, and our cinnamon meets the standards set by the Sri Lanka Standards Institution. You can also verify authenticity by the characteristic light brown color, delicate aroma, and paper-thin quill layers.
We offer several grades of Ceylon cinnamon: Alba (the finest grade with quills under 6mm diameter), C4 (7-8mm diameter), C5 (9-10mm diameter), and ground cinnamon powder. Alba grade is perfect for gourmet cooking, while C4 and C5 are excellent for everyday use. Our ground cinnamon is made from premium quills.
Store cinnamon in an airtight container away from direct sunlight, heat, and moisture. Whole cinnamon sticks can maintain their quality for up to 3-4 years when properly stored, while ground cinnamon is best used within 6-12 months. Our packaging is designed to preserve freshness, but transferring to airtight glass containers after opening is recommended.
