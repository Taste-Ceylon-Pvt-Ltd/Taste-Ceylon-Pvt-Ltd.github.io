Our Heritage

The Golden Spice of Ceylon

For centuries, Ceylon cinnamon has been treasured as "the true cinnamon" ‚Äî a spice so prized that it was once worth more than gold. Grown exclusively in the lush tropical climate of Sri Lanka, our cinnamon carries the authentic essence of this ancient tradition.

Unlike common cassia, true Ceylon cinnamon offers a delicate, complex flavor with subtle citrus notes and a naturally sweet finish. Its paper-thin layers and low coumarin content make it the choice of discerning chefs and health-conscious consumers worldwide.
