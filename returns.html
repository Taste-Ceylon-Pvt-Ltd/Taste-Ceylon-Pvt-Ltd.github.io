üìù How to Return an Item

Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at returns@tasteceylon.lk or use our contact form with:

  • Your order number
  • Reason for return
  • Photos of the product (for damaged/quality issues)

Step 2: Receive Return Authorization

We'll review your request within 24-48 hours and provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number along with return shipping instructions.

Step 3: Ship Your Return

Pack the item securely in its original packaging (if available) and ship to the address provided. Include your RMA number on the package.

Step 4: Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

  • Refund Method: Refunds are issued to your original payment method

  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Return Shipping: Customer pays return shipping for change-of-mind returns; we cover shipping for defective/incorrect items
